My Final Project

On this page is my final project for CSC 106 at the University of Rhode Island. It is a simpler version of the game show "Deal or No Deal" You can play the game right here on the web page. There is also a video of me running through the program you can watch.
Directions: Start the game by pressing the "Start Game" button. Pick cases until you are made an offer by the bank. Choose to take the offer or decline it. Continue until cases have run out or you take the deal.
